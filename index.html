About

DiploNet aims to certificate diplomas by using a blockchain technology

Today, there is around 8% of fake diplomas advertised on resumes. DiploNet is here to fix this.

The process is initiated by schools administration services on their DiploNet own space. DiploNet platform, thereafter, generates identification QR code for each diploma in order to assess their validity. Then graduates are able to guarantee their certification by reporting their QR code on resumes or other documents. By scanning the QR code the recruiters can check the authenticity of candidates background in real-time.

Benefits of DiploNet :

  • Time saving for companies in their recruitment process

  • Pledge of confidence for graduates

  • Create a link between companies and business

  • Enhance the brand awareness of schools among business

  • Add value to certification degrees

  • Cut fake and falsification of diplomas
